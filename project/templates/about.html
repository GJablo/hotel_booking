Our History

The Comfort Inn Hotel, established in 2022, has stood as a symbol of elegance and sophistication for over a year.
Originally built as a luxury retreat for affluent travelers, the hotel has since become an iconic landmark in the heart of the city.
Throughout its year of emergence, the Crowny Hotel has hosted countless dignitaries, celebrities, and discerning guests, offering unparalleled hospitality and timeless charm.
